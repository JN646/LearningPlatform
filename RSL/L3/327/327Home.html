327: Understanding Recording Techniques

Introduction

The recording process is built upon some significant concepts that are important to understand for success. Any individual who wishes to pursue a career within the production will benefit from such understanding. The basic physics of sound, microphone theory and the use of equalisation, gating and compression will not only benefit producers but will hold worth for musicians.
