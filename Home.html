Rock School: MUSPRA

Music Practitioners Qualifications are vocational, unit based qualifications which offer students the chance to learn a diverse range of music industry skills. The qualifications are available at Levels 1-3 with the opportunity to cover subjects such as performing, composing, music technology and music business. There are over 150 units to choose from across three levels.
