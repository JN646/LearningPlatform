Evaluating Your Work

Evaluating your work is the key to improvement. Where possible relate your evaluation back to your planning stage. What did you plan and did you make any changes? Try and come up with a 5 strengths and weaknesses.

Strengths

Was there anything in particular that went well? Things that you were particularly good at or understood?

Weaknesses

What didn't go so well during your project? Were they in your control or outside factors?

Areas for Improvement

If you were to do this project again what would you do and what you change? Looking back at your project did anything change from planning to production? Were there any technical issues? If so how did you solve them?
